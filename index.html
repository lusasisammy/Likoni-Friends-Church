About Us

Friends Church Quakers Likoni Monthly Meeting is part of the Nairobi Yearly Meeting of Friends Church in Kenya. We are a community of believers committed to living out the Quaker testimonies of simplicity, peace, integrity, community, equality, and stewardship.

Our History

Established in [year], Likoni Monthly Meeting has been serving the Likoni community through worship, fellowship, and various outreach programs. We trace our roots back to the early Quaker movement in Kenya and maintain strong ties with the wider Friends Church community.

Our Connection to Nairobi Yearly Meeting

As part of Nairobi Yearly Meeting, we share in the broader mission and vision of Friends Church in Kenya. The Yearly Meeting provides spiritual covering, leadership development, and coordination of joint ministries across our region.

Our Beliefs

Peace

We believe in the power of God's love to overcome violence and conflict, following Jesus' teaching to love our enemies and pursue reconciliation in all relationships.

Integrity

We strive to live lives of honesty and authenticity, letting our yes be yes and our no be no. We seek to align our actions with our beliefs in all areas of life.

Community

We value deep, authentic relationships where we support and encourage one another in our faith journeys, bearing one another's burdens and celebrating together.

Equality

We believe all people are equal before God and seek to reflect this in our church life and society. We affirm the worth and dignity of every individual.

Simplicity

We embrace a lifestyle free from excess and distraction, focusing on what is truly important. We seek to live simply so others may simply live.

Stewardship

We believe we are caretakers of God's creation, called to responsibly manage our time, talents, resources, and the environment for God's glory.
